1. Fever
* Time:
* Acute (< 1 week):
* Malaria: High fever, chills, headache, muscle aches, vomiting, diarrhea, anemia
* Typhoid: High fever, headache, weakness, loss of appetite, abdominal pain, constipation or diarrhea, rose spots on the skin
* Dengue: High fever, severe headache, muscle and joint pain, rash, bleeding gums, nosebleeds
* Viral Fever: Fever, headache, muscle aches, fatigue, sore throat, cough, runny nose
* Bacterial Infection: Fever, localized pain or redness, pus formation
* Chronic (> 1 week):
* Tuberculosis: Persistent cough, fever, night sweats, weight loss, fatigue
* HIV/AIDS: Fever, fatigue, swollen lymph nodes, weight loss, night sweats, opportunistic infections
* Malnutrition: Chronic fatigue, weakness, poor growth, muscle wasting, skin problems
* Chronic Infection: Persistent fever, fatigue, weight loss, organ dysfunction

2. Respiratory Symptoms
* Cough:
* Productive Cough:
* Pneumonia: Fever, chills, chest pain, difficulty breathing, cough with phlegm
* Tuberculosis: Persistent cough, fever, night sweats, weight loss, fatigue
* Dry Cough:
* Asthma: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, cough
* Bronchitis: Cough, chest congestion, wheezing, shortness of breath
* Shortness of Breath:
* Asthma: Wheezing, chest tightness, cough
* Pneumonia: Fever, chills, chest pain, difficulty breathing, cough with phlegm
* Heart Failure: Shortness of breath, fatigue, swelling in legs, ankles, and feet
* Chest Pain:
* Pneumonia: Fever, chills, difficulty breathing, cough with phlegm
* Heart Attack: Chest pain or discomfort, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, vomiting

3. Gastrointestinal Symptoms
* Diarrhea:
* Watery Diarrhea:
* Cholera: Severe watery diarrhea, vomiting, dehydration
* Rotavirus: Watery diarrhea, vomiting, fever, abdominal pain
* Bloody Diarrhea:
* Dysentery: Bloody diarrhea, abdominal pain, fever, mucus in stool
* Typhoid: High fever, headache, weakness, loss of appetite, abdominal pain, constipation or diarrhea, rose spots on the skin
* Abdominal Pain:
* Food Poisoning: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal cramps
* Appendicitis: Sudden, sharp abdominal pain, especially in the lower right side, loss of appetite, nausea, vomiting
* Ulcers: Burning pain in the stomach or upper abdomen, especially when the stomach is empty or after eating, nausea, vomiting, heartburn
* Vomiting:
* Food Poisoning: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal cramps
* Gastroenteritis: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal cramps, fever

4. Skin Infections
* Bacterial Infections:
* Impetigo: Red sores, blisters, or crusty patches on the skin, often around the nose and mouth
* Cellulitis: Red, swollen, painful area of skin, often warm to the touch
* Fungal Infections:
* Ringworm: Circular, scaly rash, often itchy
* Athlete's Foot: Itchy, red, scaly rash between the toes
* Viral Infections:
* Warts: Small, rough growths on the skin
* Herpes: Blisters or sores on the skin, often painful

5. Eye Infections
* Conjunctivitis: Red, itchy, watery eyes, sensitivity to light, discharge from the eyes
